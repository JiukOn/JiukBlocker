Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our absolute priority.

Total Transparency

JiukBlocker is an open and transparent extension designed strictly to improve your browsing experience by blocking ads and visual distractions. We deeply respect your privacy.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data, web browsing history, or user activity. The project is fully auditable and privacy-focused.

No data ever leaves your machine.

2. Local Storage

All your preferences (such as blocked keywords and toggle states for Ads, Spam, and Shorts) are saved locally on your device using Google Chrome's native chrome.storage.sync API.

  • The developers have absolutely no access to this data.
  • Your settings remain under your full control.

3. Permissions Justification

The extension requires access to web pages (<all_urls>) strictly to perform local, on-the-fly modifications to the website's layout (DOM) to hide intrusive elements and ads.

  • All processing happens entirely within your browser.
  • The source code is open so anyone can verify these claims.
